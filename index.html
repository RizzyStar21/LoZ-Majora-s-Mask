MORE MASKS

Below are more masks that you can find to 100% the game and trade them them in the end for a very powerful mask>

Postman's Hat

The Postman's Hat is given to Link by the Postman on the Final Day, who is distressed about wanting to flee Clock Town. After Link shows him the priority mail, he will pull himself together and quickly deliver Kafei's letter to Madame Aroma. The Postman will then meet up with Link outside the Milk Bar and give him the Postman's Hat, telling him that he has decided to flee Clock Town. The Postman will then run off, leaving Link to his important deliveries

All-Night Mask

Link can purchase the mask from the Curiosity Shop on the night of the Final Day for 500 Rupees if he prevents Sakon from getting away with the Old Lady's Bomb Bag on the night of the First Day. If he allows Sakon to steal her bag, the mask will not appear.

Blast Mask

The Blast Mask is obtained after saving the Old Lady from the Bomb Shop from being robbed. On the First Day at midnight, the Old Lady will enter North Clock Town. At exactly 12:00 am, the thief Sakon will steal her bag full of Big Bomb Bags. Link must attack him with his Sword, causing Sakon to drop the luggage and preventing him from stealing the Bomb Bags. After Sakon leaves the scene, the Old Lady will reward Link with the Blast Mask.

Stone Mask

To obtain the mask, Link must use the Lens of Truth to find the invisible soldier Shiro. After relating his story of woe, he will ask that Link give him something to cure him.[1][2] To do this, Link must give him a Red or Blue Potion; if he does, Shiro will give Link the Stone Mask in return.

Great Fairy Mask

The Great Fairy Mask is given to Link by the Great Fairy of Magic in North Clock Town. Link obtains the mask if he returns the Great Fairy's missing Stray Fairy while in human form.be During the night, it will be found in East Clock Town. Once Link brings the Stray Fairy back to the Great Fairy, she will give him the Great Fairy Mask so that he can locate Stray Fairies easily in each dungeon and return them to their respective Great Fairies.

Keaton Mask

The Keaton Mask Link receives in Majora's Mask was given to Kafei when he was little by the Man from the Curiosity Shop. Kafei wears the mask to keep his identity hidden from the residents of Clock Town. The Keaton Mask is obtained from the Man from the Curiosity Shop in Kafei's hideout, after delivering the Letter to Kafei. It requires Link to go to Kafei's hideout at 1:00 in the afternoon on the Final Day.

Bremen Mask

The Bremen Mask is given to Link by Guru-Guru. It originally belonged to a dog who was leader of an animal troupe. Link receives the Bremen Mask after listening to Guru-Guru's confession about how he stole it. However, Link will need to listen to Guru-Guru's confession on the night of the First or Second Day.

Bunny Hood

If Link equips the Bremen Mask and begins marching around the Shack, the chicks will follow him as he walks in their proximity. If Link stops his march, all of the chicks will disband. Once Link has all 10 chicks following him, they will all turn into adult Cuccos. Grog is amazed and thanks Link by giving him the Bunny Hood.

Don Gero's Mask

The mask is given to Link by a hungry Goron in the Mountain Village. To acquire the mask, Link must light all the torches lining the wall of the Goron Shrine, Goron Link must then roll off of the ramp on the top floor to collide with the pots until some Rock Sirloin falls to the floor. Link must then take the sirloin to the Mountain Village and give it to the Goron standing on a ledge. He will thank Link with Don Gero's Mask.

Mask of Scents

The Mask of Scents is given to Link by the Deku Butler after finishing his race. Link can talk to the Deku Butler, who is waiting to meet the hero in the Deku Shrine outside of the palace. Once there, he will ask Link to race him. If Link manages to keep up, the Deku Butler will say how it reminds him of racing with his son, and then gives him the Mask of Scents in return.

Romani's Mask

Romani's Mask is given to Link by Cremia. Cremia will ask if Link wants to ride with her into town while she makes a delivery. On the way, they are forced to take a detour through the Gorman Track because of a fence blocking Milk Road. Link must defend her milk supply from the Gorman Brothers, who wish to destroy it. If at least one Bottle of milk remains, Cremia will reward Link with the mask.

Circus Leader's Mask

The Troupe Leader's Mask is given to Link by Gorman inside the Milk Bar. Once Link enters by using Romani's Mask, he meets Toto, manager of the Zora band The Indigo-Go's. If Link aids him by playing the "Ballad of the Wind Fish" using the musical instruments of each of his four forms, Gorman will be moved by the tune. He then gives Link the mask to show his gratification.

Kafei's Mask

The mask is given to Link by Madame Aroma, who handmade it herself. Link must visit the Mayor's Residence on the morning of the First Day and go into Madame Aroma's office. Upon his arrival, Madame Aroma will assume Link is the professional she hired to find Kafei. She then asks if he will accept the assignment, and if he answers with "Yes," she will give him Kafei's Mask to begin his search.

Couple's Mask

The Couple's Mask is obtained by completing the reuniting Kafei and Anju sidequest. The Couple's Mask is different from other masks in that it gives no special ability or attributes when worn and has very little use. If it is worn in the Mayor's Residence during a meeting in any of the three days, Mayor Dotour will end the meeting and give Link a Piece of Heart.

Mask of Truth

n Majora's Mask, the Mask of Truth is given to Link by the Cursed Man in the Swamp Spider House. According to him, he was told that he could become rich if he were to look inside, and was then given the mask. However, he became cursed as he went inside, and was turned into a Skulltula-like creature. He tasks Link with defeating the 30 Gold Skulltulas in the house to lift the curse, and rewards him with the Mask of Truth as thanks.

Kamaro's Mask

Kamaro's Mask is given to Link by the spirit of Kamaro. Kamaro appears in Termina Field near the Snowhead area on top of a mushroom-like tree. He appears between midnight and 4 AM on the First or Second Day, where some music can be heard. Kamaro is afraid that his dance moves will never be passed down to the new generation, and asks that Link rest his soul. By playing the "Song of Healing", his musical talent will be encased inside the Mask. He entrusts the hero with his final request, to spread his dance across the world.

Gibdo Mask

The Gibdo Mask is given to Link by Pamela's Father after he lifts the curse cast upon Ikana River in Ikana Canyon. Upon entering the Music Box House, the hero finds Pamela's Father in a state of half-mummification. By playing the "Song of Healing," her father will be healed, and the mummified portion of him encased in a mask.

Garo's Mask

The Mask is given to Link by the Gorman Brothers. To obtain it, he must go to the Gorman Track once he has Epona, and ask to race against them for 10 Rupees. If Link wins, they will award him with the Garo's Mask. Wearing it allows entry into Ikana Canyon, as well as the ability to encounter and battle Garo Ninjas. If defeated, the Garo Ninja will reward Link with a tip that may help him on his quest.

Captians Hat

The Captain's Hat is obtained after Link awakens and defeats Captain Keeta. Link must awaken Captain Keeta from where he rests near the Ikana Graveyard using the "Sonata of Awakening." After he defeats Captain Keeta, he will submit to Link's power and After making his final request, he shows the hero a Treasure Chest containing the Captain's Hat and takes his leave. Link must then use the Hookshot or jump across the bridge guardrails to claim the mask.

Giant's Mask

When Link wears the mask, he becomes a giant. It is only usable in Twinmold's chamber at the Stone Tower Temple. Wearing the mask will drain Link's Magic Meter, and if depleted, he will return to normal size. In Majora's Mask 3D, the mask also allows Link to attack with his bare hands, permitting Link to do uppercuts, ground slams, and spin the boss around In Majora's Mask, the Giant's Mask is obtained in the Stone Tower Temple after defeating an Eyegore
